FRENCH FRIES RECIPE

Steps:

  1. Soak potato strips in a large bowl of water for about 30 minutes.
  2. Overhead view of a bowl of cut fries for potatoes soaking in water,Pat with paper towels until thoroughly dry.
  3. Overhead shot of a baking sheet with cut fries laying on paper towels
  4. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 275 degrees F (135 degrees C). Gently add potatoes to the hot oil and fry for about 5 minutes, stirring and flipping the potatoes occasionally.
  5. High angle view of fries cooking in hot oil on the stove,Use a slotted spoon to transfer potatoes to a paper towel-lined plate. Let cool completely.
  6. High angle view of par-cooked fries on a baking sheet lined with paper towels
  7. Heat oil again, but this time to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Add potatoes and fry a second time until golden brown, 5 to 6 minutes.
  8. High angle view of cut potatoes cooking in a pan of hot oil to make fries.Remove from the deep-fryer and blot with a paper towel. Sprinkle with salt to serve.
